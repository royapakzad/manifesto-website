How to Support

Join us in advocating for internet freedom and digital rights. Your support helps us continue our mission to ensure unrestricted access to information and communication for all.

Ways to Support Our Cause

  • Share our manifesto and raise awareness about internet censorship
  • Follow us on social media and amplify our message
  • Contact your representatives about digital rights policies
  • Support organizations working for internet freedom
  • Use and promote privacy tools and secure communication platforms
  • Advocate for digital rights in your community and networks
  • Document and report internet shutdowns and digital rights violations
  • Educate others about the importance of internet freedom

Spread the Word

Help us reach more people by sharing our manifesto and mission. Every voice matters in the fight for digital freedom. Use hashtags like #InternetFreedom, #WomanLifeFreedom, and #DigitalRights when sharing our content.

Advocate for Policy Change

Contact your local representatives and international organizations to advocate for:

  • Recognition of internet shutdowns as human rights violations
  • International accountability for digital censorship
  • Support for circumvention technologies
  • Democratic governance of satellite internet infrastructure

Connect with Us

Follow our work and stay updated on internet freedom issues. Your engagement helps amplify our voice and brings attention to the struggles of those fighting for digital rights in Iran and around the world.
